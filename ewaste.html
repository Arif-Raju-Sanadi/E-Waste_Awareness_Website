E-Waste – The Global Picture

E-waste refers to discarded electrical or electronic devices. It is the fastest-growing waste stream worldwide.

According to the Global E-waste Monitor 2024, in 2022:

  • 62 million tonnes generated
  • Only 22.3 % (≈13.8 Mt) formally recycled

Source: ewastemonitor.info
